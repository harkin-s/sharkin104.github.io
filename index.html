Final Year Project

Partner Selection in Evolutionary Computation

The aim of this project is to explore the performance of partner selection algorithms in the field of evolutionary computation, with a particular focus on their use in Genetic algorithms. Several methods were tested for their ability to perform on a range of problem landscapes of varying difficulty, this also explored the effect of modifying partner selection methods and their impact on performance .

The project involved the development of a genetic algorithm capable of using a number of different partner selection methods and testing them on problem landscapes of varying difficulty. The program allowed me to quickly change the parameters of a test and gather and interpret the results of each experiment. The program was written in C#
